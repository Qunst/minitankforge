Core Soviet Armor

The T-34 and T-34/85 are natural starting points for Soviet forces. SU-series vehicles add support and anti-armor roles, while KV and IS heavy tanks bring more table presence for breakthrough and late-war scenarios.

Useful Pairings

A practical Soviet collection can mix T-34s, SU-85 or SU-100 tank destroyers, ISU assault guns, and one or two heavy tanks. The set pages are useful when you want a ready-made group.
