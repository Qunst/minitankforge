About MiniTankForge

MiniTankForge is run by Davorin, from Croatia, and grew from a personal hobby into a small extra source of income for the home, built around miniature tanks, tabletop gaming, and a real love for the hobby side of things.

The story behind it

What started as a hobby became something more

MiniTankForge began as a hobby project: making miniature tanks, learning what works, improving prints, and enjoying the process of turning small ideas into something tangible for the tabletop.

Over time, it grew into a small business that helps bring a bit of extra profit into the home. The site is still built with that same hobby mindset, though. It is meant to feel personal, focused, and honest rather than mass-produced or anonymous.

Why this site exists

A warmer, clearer place to browse

Marketplace listings are useful for checkout, but they are not always the best place to explain scale, finish options, set contents, or the difference between direct requests and Etsy.

MiniTankForge exists to make browsing easier, show more examples, and help people feel more confident before they decide what they want.

MiniTankForge does not handle direct checkout. You can contact me with a request and pay by PayPal after details are confirmed, or continue to Etsy if you prefer.
How the tanks are made

Resin printed for detail and durability

The tanks are made through resin printing. The resin used is ABS-like, which helps make the models tougher and more resistant to damage than more brittle prints.

Some models are built from scratch, while others start from simpler models found online and are then heavily modified, refined, and adapted into something more usable and appealing for miniature gaming.

What keeps growing

New game sets are part of the plan

After being introduced to Mike Lambo's games, I started making game sets as well, not just individual tanks. That opened up a new side of the project and gave me more ideas for complete tabletop-ready groups.

I plan to keep making new sets over time, alongside new individual tanks, so the site continues to grow in a way that feels useful for both players and collectors.

Seen in play

Mike Lambo has featured my tanks

My tanks have also appeared in videos connected to Mike Lambo's games, which has been a meaningful encouragement for this project and for the game-set side of the range.

Personal, practical, and easy to trust
