German WW2 Tank Miniatures

Browse German WW2 armor for tabletop games, from Panzer workhorses to heavy tanks, assault guns, and tank destroyers.

MiniTankForge is browse-only. Use this page to compare German vehicles, then send a direct request or continue to Etsy from the product page.

What Fits German WW2 Forces

A German force can be built around familiar medium tanks like the Panzer IV and Panther, then expanded with heavier vehicles such as Tiger I and Tiger II. Tank destroyers and assault guns add useful variety, especially for defensive scenarios and compact hex-based games.

Choosing Scale and Finish

Most individual German vehicles are available across the standard MiniTankForge scale range. Use the scale comparison page for size context, and the finish guide to compare unpainted models with base coat options.
