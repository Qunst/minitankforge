HOW THIS WORKS
Browse on MiniTankForge, then choose direct contact or Etsy checkout
Important

MiniTankForge is for browsing, not direct checkout

This website helps you browse tanks and sets more clearly, compare scale, and understand what each listing includes. Customers do not place orders directly on MiniTankForge.

Option A: contact me directly about the item you want, then complete payment separately
Option B: use the Etsy listing and complete checkout on Etsy
Step 1

Browse tanks and sets here

Use MiniTankForge to explore available models, compare options, check scale, and understand whether a page shows a single tank or a set.

Step 2

Choose how you want to order

Once you know what you want, you can either contact me directly about the item or continue through Etsy checkout.

Option A

Contact me directly

If you want to order directly, contact me with the tank or set you want, together with any needed details such as scale, finish, or quantity.

Browse here → send your request directly → receive payment instructions → order is confirmed after payment

Direct payments can be completed separately, for example by PayPal or bank transfer, depending on what is agreed.

Option B

Buy through Etsy

If you prefer Etsy, use the Etsy button on the product page. That takes you to the corresponding Etsy listing, where checkout is completed on Etsy.

Browse here → open the Etsy listing → complete checkout on Etsy
