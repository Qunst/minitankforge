HOW THIS WORKS
Browse on MiniTankForge, complete your purchase on Etsy
Important

MiniTankForge is not a webshop

This website is here to help you browse tanks and sets more clearly, compare scale, and understand what each listing includes. Actual ordering, payment, and checkout happen on Etsy.

You do not buy directly on MiniTankForge.
When you are ready to order, use the Etsy button on the product page and complete checkout there.
Go to Etsy Shop
Step 1

Browse tanks and sets here

Use MiniTankForge to view available models, see clearer presentation, compare options, and understand whether a page shows a single tank or a set.

Step 2

Open the Etsy listing

When you find the item you want, use the Etsy button on that page. That takes you to the correct Etsy listing for the actual order.

Step 3

Complete checkout on Etsy

Payment, cart, shipping details, and order completion are all handled on Etsy, not on this site.

Why it works this way

Better browsing, familiar checkout

Etsy is used for the actual purchase, while MiniTankForge gives more room for product browsing, scale explanation, and clearer presentation than Etsy alone.

Good to know

No cart on this website

You cannot add products to a cart or pay directly on MiniTankForge. This site is for browsing and product explanation only.

If you want to place an order, always use the Etsy link on the relevant item page.
Need something specific?

Requests still start here

If the tank you want is not listed yet, you can use the Tank Requests page first. Once a model is available, ordering still happens through Etsy.
