Scale guide

1:285 WW2 Tank Miniatures

1:285 is a compact scale for tabletop tank games, scenario packs, and larger vehicle groups on smaller boards.

Many MiniTankForge single tanks can be selected in 1:285. Game-ready packs such as Hellcat Tankers are built around fixed scale play.

Why Choose 1:285?

This scale is useful when you want more vehicles on the table without needing a large play area. It works well for compact hex-based play, squadron-sized groups, and scenario packs where the overall force matters as much as each individual vehicle.

How To Compare Size

If you are unsure whether 1:285 is the right size, use the scale comparison page before choosing. It shows miniature tanks in context so scale decisions are easier than judging from product photos alone.
