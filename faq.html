FAQ

This page exists to reduce misunderstandings before Etsy checkout.

Is a single tank page for one tank or multiple tanks?

Single tank pages are for one tank unless the page clearly states otherwise.

Are sets handled separately?

Yes. Sets have their own browsing page and their own detail pages.

Can I choose scale?

Yes. Scale is visible while browsing and can be adjusted again on product pages.

Where do purchases happen?

Purchases still go through Etsy. This site supports browsing and clarity.

What finish options exist?

This skeleton shows base coat and unpainted as example finish choices.

What if I need a tank that isn’t listed?

Use the Tank Requests page to ask for a specific unavailable tank.
