Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

Quick answers about browsing, finish options, direct requests, and ordering.

Can I order directly on this site?

No. MiniTankForge is a browse-only site. When you know what you want, you can contact me directly with your request or continue to Etsy.

Can I request an order directly and pay by PayPal?

Yes. If you contact me directly with the tank or set you want, payment can be completed by PayPal after the details are confirmed.

Is Etsy still an option?

Yes. You can still use Etsy if you prefer marketplace checkout. This site simply makes browsing, comparing, and understanding the products easier first.

Is a single tank page for one tank or for multiple tanks?

A single tank page is for one tank unless the page clearly states otherwise.

Can I choose scale and finish?

Yes. Available scale and finish options are shown while browsing and again on the product pages.

What is the difference between unpainted and base coat?

Unpainted models are raw prints for hobbyists who want to do their own finishing. Base coat models receive a simple first paint layer for a cleaner starting point. See the Finish Guide page for examples.

Are sets separate from individual tanks?

Yes. Sets have their own browse page and detail pages, and each set page lists exactly what is included.

What if I need a tank that is not listed yet?

Use the Tank Requests page and send me the vehicle name, plus any useful details such as scale, finish, or references.
