Scale guide

1:250 WW2 Tank Miniatures

1:250 gives compact tabletop tanks a little more presence while still keeping board space practical.

The Lone Sherman game packs are fixed at 1:250, and many single tanks can also be selected in 1:250 from their product pages.

Why Choose 1:250?

1:250 is a friendly middle ground for small games: still compact, but with slightly more visual size than 1:285. It works well when you want recognizable vehicles, clear silhouettes, and enough room for scenario play.

Finish Options

Unpainted models are useful for hobbyists who want full control. Base coat options are helpful when you want a cleaner starting point before detail painting or tabletop use.
