Model notes

The M3 Lee adds an unusual Allied medium tank profile with its side-mounted main gun and tall superstructure. It is a strong pick for early-war, North Africa, lend-lease, or transitional force collections where visual variety is part of the appeal.

Historical note

Its unusual multi-level gun layout gave the M3 Lee one of the most distinctive profiles of any Allied tank.
