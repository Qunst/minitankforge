Model notes

The M26 Pershing gives late-war USA forces a heavier tank option with a different presence from the Sherman family. It is a good choice for end-of-war scenarios, stronger Allied armor groups, and collections that reach into the transition toward post-war tank design.

Historical note

Arriving late in the war, the Pershing was one of the first American tanks designed to face heavier German armor on more equal terms.
