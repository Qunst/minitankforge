Model notes

The A-32 is a Soviet prototype model for collectors who enjoy development vehicles and the path toward the T-34. It works well in what-if scenarios, prototype displays, and comparison lineups beside early T-34 designs.

Historical note

The A-32 was an important Soviet prototype that helped lead toward the famous T-34 design.
