Model notes

The Sd.Kfz. 234 adds fast German armored-car character to reconnaissance, screening, and mobile support scenarios. Its eight-wheeled layout gives a collection a different shape language from tracked vehicles and helps break up tank-heavy lineups.

Historical note

The Sd.Kfz. 234 family gave German units a fast, eight-wheeled armored car for reconnaissance and mobile support.
