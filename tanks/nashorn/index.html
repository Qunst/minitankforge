Model notes

The Nashorn is a lightly protected German tank destroyer built around long-range firepower, making it useful for open-table ambushes and rear-line support. Its tall fighting compartment and exposed gun profile make it visually different from low casemate vehicles like the Hetzer.

Historical note

Lightly protected but strongly armed, the Nashorn relied on long-range firepower rather than armor to survive.
