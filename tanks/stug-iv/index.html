Model notes

The StuG IV is a German assault gun that fits well into late-war support roles and mixed defensive formations. Its Panzer IV-based hull makes it a good companion to Panzer IV and Jagdpanzer IV models while still adding a different battlefield purpose.

Historical note

The StuG IV used the Panzer IV chassis to keep assault-gun production going when StuG III manufacturing was disrupted.
