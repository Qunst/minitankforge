Model notes

The ISU-122 is a Soviet heavy tank destroyer that brings the ISU casemate shape into a more anti-armor focused role. It is a good companion to the ISU-152 when building heavy Soviet support groups with different battlefield jobs.

Historical note

The ISU-122 used the heavy IS chassis with a 122 mm gun for long-range fire support and anti-armor work.
