Model notes

The Type 95 Ha-Go is a small Japanese light tank that suits Pacific, early-war, and island campaign scenarios. Its compact size and distinctive profile help Japanese armored forces feel different from German, Soviet, and American lineups.

Historical note

Light, mobile, and widely used, the Ha-Go became one of Imperial Japan's best-known armored vehicles.
