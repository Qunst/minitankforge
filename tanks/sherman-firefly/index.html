Model notes

The Sherman Firefly is an Allied medium tank with a long-gun profile that immediately separates it from standard Shermans. It is useful for British and Commonwealth forces, tank-hunter roles, and scenarios where Allied armor needs a stronger answer to German heavies.

Historical note

By mounting the 17-pounder gun, the Firefly turned the Sherman into one of the most effective Allied tank killers in Northwest Europe.
